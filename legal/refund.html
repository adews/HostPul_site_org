REfund Policy

Introduction

Our policy lasts 7 days. If 7 days have gone by since your first purchase with us, unfortunately we can’t offer you a refund.

To be eligible for a refund after a service has been activated, you must request a refund during the 7-day period. During this time, you can cancel the service for 100% of your money back.

Please reach out to sales if you aren't sure about what you are purchasing. If you are experiencing difficulties or performance related issues, please reach out to support.

Refunds (if applicable)

Once your refund request is received, we will send you an email to let you know that we have received request. We will also let you know of the approval or rejection of your refund.

If you are approved, then your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your credit card or original method of payment, within a certain amount of days.

Cancellation refunds

We DO NOT refund partial monthly fees to accounts. We require 30 days’ notice for a cancellation. In order to receive a refund you must open a ticket.

Late or missing refunds (if applicable)

If you haven’t received a refund yet, first check your bank account again.

Then contact your bank and check if any refunds are pending.

If you’ve done all of this and you still have not received your refund yet, please open a billing related ticket. We will contact you during normal business hours.

Sale items (if applicable)

Only regular priced items may be refunded, unfortunately sale items cannot be refunded.
